WHY NBOTI TECH INITIATIVE?

Introducing basic school learners to Robotics, AI and STEM education. Currently training 350+ students across the Upper East Region.

We seek to partner with Proprietors or Headtechers to deliver STEM/Robotics lessons for students at a fee.

We begun as a weekend class at the Great Victroy E-leraning center since 2024, and have fast incorporated into 4 schools as a fully running curriculum.

Our Leraners are excited that they are able to create amazing things from scratch.
