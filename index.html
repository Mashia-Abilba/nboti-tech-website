WHY NBOTI TECH INITIATIVE?

This is an initiative that has been established with the goal of introducing young and great minds from basic level, to real life technology, innovation and problem-solving skills.

This Initiative is meant to get basic school learners to understand some of the concepts they learn in Mathematics, Integrated Science and Computing and relate them to real life situations. Hence, the introduction of Robotics.

Robotics is a branch of engineering that deals with designing, programming, constructing, operating and using machines to solve human needs in health, manufacturing, agriculture, construction etc.

Considering the Educational curriculum, my team and I believe that nurturing these young ones towards becoming problem solvers, rather than people waiting to be employed is the best way forward.

You would agree with me, that the kinds of problems you are able to solve in the society determines the level of impact and wealth you will have.
